Clay Station 7 Triumphs at Designers' Competition

Clay Station 7 recently claimed the prestigious winning trophy at the national Designers' Competition for their extraordinary cement sculpture of a mother elephant and her baby. This powerful piece, which embodies the spirit of Namibia, captured the judges' attention for its intricate detail and profound symbolism.

Elephants, iconic to the Namibian landscape, symbolize strength, family, and protection, mirroring the values of this vibrant nation. The sculpture was lauded not only for its artistic excellence but also for its deep connection to the country’s natural heritage.

This masterpiece was showcased at the National Art Gallery of Namibia, where it drew widespread admiration from art lovers and visitors alike. The exhibition highlighted the creative prowess of Clay Station 7 and their ability to weave cultural narratives into their cement creations. This victory reinforces the team’s status as leaders in innovative, handcrafted decorative art.
